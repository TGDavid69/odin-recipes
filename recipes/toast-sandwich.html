Toast Sandwich!

Description :3

The toast sandwich is a luxurious dish available only to the richest of the U.K's (United Kingdom) elite. By stacking not one, or two, but
three pieces of bread upon each other and toasting the middle piece, one creates a captivating dish that is both pleasing to the eyes and palette.

Ingredients :0

To make a proper toast sandwich, one will need the following:

  • A slice of bread
  • A slice of toast (very important)
  • A slice of bread

Steps :8

Despite the dish's mythical status, once the ingredients are assembled, you are only minutes away from experiencing utter bliss.

  1. Put one of the three slices of bread in a toaster
  2. While the center slice is toasting, grab one slice of bread and place it on a plate
  3. Wait for the toaster to convert the bread to toast (approximately two minutes)
  4. Now that the toast is ready, place it on top of your first bread slice.
  5. Finally, place the final piece of bread atop the toast and enjoy!
